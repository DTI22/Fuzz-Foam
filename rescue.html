CAT RESCUE



Since 2022, Fuzz & Foam cat rescue has been a safe haven for homeless cats. We make a promise to any cat who comes through our doors: they will never be homeless again. We hope our feline friends will find a loving home with a lifelong companion or a loving family; but if they never find that home, they are welcomed into the Fuzz & Foam family.

While our main goal is to find a responsible home for our residents, we work hard to ensure all of our furbabies recieve the level of care they need. Thanks to the help of our loyal staff and volunteers, all cats can find a loving home here. We are always looking for compassionate people to donate their time and help us in our mission to care for our residents.

In addition to being a safe haven for our feline friends, Fuzz & Foam offers free or discounted emergency medical services. We also offer low cost spay and neuter clinics to the surrounding community.

Why we have animal shelters?


Animal shelters are a vital part of our society. Animal shelters are places where animals can go when they are not being taken care of by their owners or when they have been abandoned. Animals in shelters may be able to find a home, or they may be sent to another shelter that will take care of them until they can be adopted. Animal shelters often have programs that help the animals stay healthy and happy, and they also provide socialization opportunities for the animals. They also work to stop animal cruelty and promote animal welfare. We all must support our local animal shelters, both financially and by spreading the word about their good work.


Animal shelters save countless lives by providing shelter to animals who would otherwise be euthanized. They also provide a place for animals to stay while waiting for their new home or until their owner can bring them back. In addition, animal shelters provide education about animals and the importance of responsible pet ownership. This makes them an important part of the community.
